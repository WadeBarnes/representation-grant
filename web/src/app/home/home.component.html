Representation Grants

Use this service to apply for a Representation Grant from the Provincial Court of British Columbia.

Time: The average time to complete this service is 30min. If the Representation Grant is urgent, you may get it the same day you file the application at a courthouse. A non-urgent Representation Grant will take more time.

Cost: This service is free. The Government of British Columbia does not charge fees for Family Law cases in the Provincial Court of British Columbia.

Print: You will need a printer to print your application.
